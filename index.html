Calculation Notes

Base case logic

Revenue Model

  • Hardware units start from the 2026 target, currently 3,000 units.
  • Hardware revenue = annual units sold x average hardware price.
  • Subscription revenue = average active subscribers x annual subscription fee.
  • Total revenue = hardware revenue + subscription revenue.

Subscriber Dynamics

  • New subscribers are assumed to equal annual hardware units sold.
  • Ending subscribers = beginning subscribers - churned subscribers + new subscribers.
  • Average subscribers = (beginning subscribers + ending subscribers) / 2.
  • Churn follows the Excel base case: no launch-year churn, then 10%, 15%, and 15%.

Base Assumptions

  • Forecast starts in 2026 because 2025 has passed.
  • 2026 sales target is 3,000 units.
  • 2027-2029 penetration follows the Excel base case: 0.20%, 0.50%, and 1.20% of 30 million target households.
  • Hardware price, COGS, subscription fee, and subscription COGS use the Excel base case unless changed in the controls.

Profit & Opex

  • Hardware gross profit = hardware revenue - units sold x hardware COGS.
  • Subscription gross profit = subscription revenue x (1 - subscription COGS %).
  • Operating expenses = team + sales & marketing + R&D + G&A + support.
  • Operating profit = gross profit - operating expenses.
